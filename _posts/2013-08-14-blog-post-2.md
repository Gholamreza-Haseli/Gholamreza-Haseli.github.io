Attending the **52nd International Conference on Computer & Industrial Engineering (CIE52)
Lyon, France** 🇫🇷 | October 29–31, 2025.

Last week, I had an amazing opportunity to join CIE52, where brilliant minds from academia and industry came together to share insights and innovations shaping the future of computer and industrial engineering. 

I was honored to present our paper,
**“A Scenario-based Stratified Base-Criterion Method for Assessing the Blockchain Technology Adoption Barriers in Retail Supply Chain”.**

Thanks to my co-authors **Dr. Mostafa Hajiaghaei-Keshteli**, **Prof. Muhammet Deveci**, and **Dr. Hana Tomášková**.
I also had the pleasure of meeting and speaking personally with several keynote speakers, including Professors Reza Zanjirani Farahani, Madjid Tavana, Daria Battini, Christoph Glock, and Lynette Cheah. Their insights and advice were truly inspiring and will definitely influence my future research directions.
A huge thanks also to Professors Yasser Dessouky, Masood Fathi, Taha Arbaoui, and Khaled Hadj-Hamou for their leadership and organization. Your effort in bringing together such a vibrant research community is deeply appreciated. 🙏
