I am truly delighted to share that I have just received the hard copies of our recently published book, Reliable Decision-Making for Sustainable Transportation, released by [Elsevier](https://www.elsevier.com) – Academic Press.

This project began during my involvement in the VOTE TRA project at [University College Dublin](https://www.ucd.ie). I am deeply grateful to Dr. Mostafa Hajiaghaei-Keshteli (my supervisor) and Dr. Sarbast Moslem (PI of the project) for their invaluable support throughout this journey.

A very special thanks goes to the **46 contributing authors** of the **18 chapters** of this volume. Your expertise and dedication made this book a comprehensive and diverse contribution to the field of sustainable transportation. I am also thankful to my home institutions, [Tecnológico de Monterrey](https://tec.mx/en) and [University College Dublin](https://www.ucd.ie), for their support in making this truly an inter-university and collaborative endeavor.

Finally, I am pleased to note that the book is now available through [Elsevier](https://www.sciencedirect.com/book/edited-volume/9780443337406/reliable-decision-making-for-sustainable-transportation). I warmly encourage colleagues interested in sustainable transportation and decision-making methodologies to request or access the book from Elsevier.

Link to download:
[Reliable Decision Making For Sustainable Transportation](https://www.sciencedirect.com/book/edited-volume/9780443337406/reliable-decision-making-for-sustainable-transportation)
