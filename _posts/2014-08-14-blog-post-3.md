I’m excited to share our latest publication, “**Cloud Supply Chain as a Service: Overcoming Barriers in the Fashion Retail Industry by Developing a New Cognitive Map Model**,” in Transportation Research Part E: Logistics and Transportation Review (**ABDC: A*** | **ABS: 3** | **CiteScore: 15.0** | **IF: 8.8**). 
https://lnkd.in/eepR-KzH

This paper is part of my research on Cloud Supply Chain as a Service under the supervision of Dr. Mostafa Hajiaghaei-Keshteli at Tecnológico de Monterrey. In this research, we examine the adoption barriers of cloud supply chain as a service in the fashion retail sector, and present a robust decision-support model to overcome them. Additionally, this paper introduces a novel fuzzy cognitive map model within a reliable multi-level group decision analytics framework. 

I am grateful to my supervisor, Dr. Mostafa Hajiaghaei-Keshteli, and to my advisors, Prof. Muhammet Deveci and Dr. Hana Tomášková, for their continued guidance throughout this research journey.
