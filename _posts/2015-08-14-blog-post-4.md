I am truly delighted to share that I have just received the hard copies of our recently published book, **Reliable Decision-Making for Sustainable Transportation**, released by **Elsevier – Academic Press**.

<img src="/assets/images/posts/book-reliable.jpeg" alt="Reliable Decision-Making Book" style="width:100%; border-radius:10px; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom: 20px;">

This project began during my involvement in the **VOTE TRA project** at **University College Dublin**. I am deeply grateful to **Dr. Mostafa Hajiaghaei-Keshteli** (my supervisor) and **Dr. Sarbast Moslem** (PI of the project) for their invaluable support throughout this journey.

A very special thanks goes to the **46 contributing authors** of the 18 chapters of this volume. Your expertise and dedication made this book a comprehensive and diverse contribution to the field of sustainable transportation. I am also thankful to my home institutions, **Tecnológico de Monterrey** and **University College Dublin**, for their support in making this truly an inter-university and collaborative endeavor.

Finally, I am pleased to note that the book is now available through Elsevier. I warmly encourage colleagues interested in sustainable transportation and decision-making methodologies to request or access the book from Elsevier.

Key Topics Covered:
======

Advanced Decision-Making
------
Integration of the **Base Criterion Method (BCM)** in transportation planning.

Sustainability in Logistics
------
Addressing environmental and operational challenges in modern transportation systems.
