📘 Now Available for Pre-Order in **[Elsevier | Encyclopedia of Multi-Attribute Decision Making (MADM)](https://shop.elsevier.com/books/encyclopedia-of-multi-attribute-decision-making-madm/haseli/978-0-443-33275-3)**

I am pleased to share that the Encyclopedia of Multi-Attribute Decision Making (MADM), published by **Elsevier**, is now available for pre-order, with the official release scheduled for **August 1, 2026**. I had the honor to serve as the lead editor of this international reference work.

This encyclopedia has been developed with a clear objective to provide a foundational and authoritative educational resource for learning and teaching MADM methods.

🔹 International scope and academic credibility

✔️ **100 chapters, each dedicated to a single MADM method**

✔️ **Contributions from 141 researchers**

✔️ **Authors affiliated with 94 universities across 36 countries, reflecting a truly global collaboration**

✔️ **Several chapters are authored by the original developers of the methods, significantly strengthening the scientific reliability of the encyclopedia**

🔹 Designed for education and research
Each chapter presents the corresponding MADM method in a clear, structured, and educational manner, supported by numerical examples, making the encyclopedia suitable as both a teaching reference and a research handbook.

This project represents a collective effort of the international decision-making community, and I hope it will serve as a long-term reference for researchers, educators, and practitioners worldwide. I would like to express my sincere appreciation to my fellow editors, Prof. Muhammet Deveci and Dr. Mostafa Hajiaghaei-Keshteli, for their outstanding collaboration, as well as to all contributing authors for their valuable chapters. This encyclopedia would not have been possible without the collective efforts of such a distinguished international group of researchers.
